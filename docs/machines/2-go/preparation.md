## Step A - Heat-Set Inserts

First, heat-set the threaded inserts (using a soldering iron) into the printed parts that require them.
Here is an overview of which insert goes where:

- Funnel: One M2.5 on the top connecting point
- Pump Grid: eight M2.5 on the pump mounting points, two M3 on the sides of the top, two M4 on the extensions coming from the grid
- Main Board: one M3 on the fillet facing front, eight M2.5 on the electric mounting points
- Leg Holder: Two M4 in the plain face facing outside, four M2.5 on the electric mounting points facing inside in ONE of both leg holders
